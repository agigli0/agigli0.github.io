The physics behind the app only hold for velocities under Mach 1 and altitudes inside the troposphere. This is meant as a visualization tool to help users understand the effects that certain factors have on air resistance, not as a modeling tool for military-grade hardware or rocket science. If you are doing those things, please don't use a college freshman's introductory computer science project.

I wrote this as my final project for CS50 at Harvard in my freshman year. This was my first complete programming project.
